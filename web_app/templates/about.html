Unified Interface Architecture

In the **immunOS** ecosystem, we treat Digital Signals (AI text/code) and Biological Signals (EEG/ECG) through the same mathematical lens.

Just as a biological immune system uses universal "detect and respond" logic, Antigence™ uses **Negative Selection** to classify any data stream as either "Self" (trusted truth) or "Non-Self" (hallucinations or threats).

Equation 20 ($R_q > R_{self}$)

$R_q > R_{self}$

The Binary Threshold for Scientific Truth

This is the **Negative Selection Binding Rule**. It is the formal filter that determines scientific validity:

  • $R_q$ (Query Affinity): How closely a new signal matches an immune detector.
  • $R_{self}$ (Self Threshold): The boundary of known, validated truths.

A detector only fires if it falls outside the self-space. This is the **Zero-Trust formula** for high-stakes research.
