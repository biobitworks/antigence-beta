# Common grammar errors to detect
Data shows (should be: Data show)
This data (should be: These data)
Less participants (should be: Fewer participants)
Between the three groups (should be: Among the three groups)
Very unique (redundant)
Quite excellent (redundant)
Firstly (should be: First)
Secondly (should be: Second)
In regards to (should be: With regard to)
Different than (should be: Different from)
Due to the fact that (wordy: should be: Because)
In order to (wordy: should be: To)
At this point in time (wordy: should be: Now)
